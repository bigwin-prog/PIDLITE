About Pidilite

Since its inception in 1959, Pidilite Industries Limited has been a pioneer in consumer and speciality chemicals in India.

Pioneers in Adhesives, Waterproofing, Pigments, and More

As a customer-centric company, we listened to our consumers and thus have been able to bring quality and innovation to your life. Whether it is small or large applications, in the home or for industry, we are dedicated to making products and solutions that offer lifelong reliability.

Today, we have carved a space for ourselves in households and across industries. Our ground-breaking adhesives are market leaders. Our reach has expanded across borders and shores, making Pidilite products accessible to various markets, demographics, geographies, and people from all walks of life.
